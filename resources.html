Student Debt Resources

Check out these helpful links about student debt!

Student Debt Crisis Center

The Student Debt Crisis Center is a nonprofit organization that fights to help borrowers with their debt and advocate for change through national and state legislatures


Click this link to sign their petition that has over 1.2 million signatures.

Student Loan Debt Statistics

The Education Data Initiative collects statistics about student debt in various demographics in order to inform people about how the student debt crisis affects different types of people.

National Conference of State Legislatures

The National Conference of State Legislatures represents legilslatures in the states and territories of the US. It works to improve operation and management of the state legilsatures, while encouraging greater cooperation between state and federal legislatures.

New America

New America is a platform for creating solutions to common problems within the US. They surface, share, and scale locally generated and tested solutions to public problems through their platform. They do this by helping develop legal, political and technological tools to enable solutions to spread and be implemented.
